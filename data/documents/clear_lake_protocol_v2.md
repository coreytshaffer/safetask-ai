# Clear Lake Sampling Protocol

When collecting water samples at Clear Lake, especially near the North Shore, always record exact GPS coordinates.
Samples must be kept below 4°C during transport.
If cyanobacteria blooms are visible, field staff must wear appropriate PPE (gloves and eye protection) and avoid direct skin contact with the water.
