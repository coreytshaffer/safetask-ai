# Section 402: Harmful Algal Blooms

A waterbody is considered out of compliance if cyanotoxin concentrations exceed 0.8 µg/L for microcystins.
Local agencies must post public health warnings if a visual bloom is confirmed alongside toxin exceedance.
Field observers cannot issue public health warnings based solely on visual observations; lab confirmation is legally required.
